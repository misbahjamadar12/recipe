Chocolate cake

This chocolate cake recipe is a classic. This version is fast and quick and your kids will love it.

Difficulty Preparation Cooking Total time
Easy 10 min 25 min 35 min

Ingredients

  • Dark Chocolate: 200g
  • Butter: 300g
  • Sugar: 150g
  • Flour: 80g
  • Eggs: 4

Instructions

  1. Melt chocolate and the butter together
  2. Mix the sugar,eggs and the flour
  3. Mix everything to get a smooth dough
  4. Bake in the oven
